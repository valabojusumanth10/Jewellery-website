Designer Jewellery

Jewelry created by a well-known,established jewelry designer.
This type of jewelry is often made from high-quality materials
and is designed to be both stylish and functional.

Some of the world's most famous jewelry designers
include Cartier, Van Cleef & Arpels, and Tiffany & Co. Designer
jewelry is often more expensive than non-designer jewelry.
Designer jewelry can range from simple, classic pieces
to more elaborate and statement-making designs.
